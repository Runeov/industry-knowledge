About the team

We are a four-person team built around clear roles: a Scrum Master keeping ceremonies tight, a Developer turning stories into working code, a QA holding the quality bar, and a Designer making the work calm and usable.

This site was produced in a one-week sprint as part of Noroff's Industry Knowledge project. The brief was deliberately small so the focus could land on the agile process itself: planning, daily stand-ups, paired review, and a retrospective at the end.

The public page you are reading is intentionally minimal. Content lives in plain Markdown files and is editable through a small Netlify CMS admin at /admin/, so non-developers on the team can update copy without touching the codebase.
